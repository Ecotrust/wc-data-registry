Contribute

Do you have resources pertaining to the West Coast that you think could be valuable to others? If so, we encourage you to fill out the form below and we will contact you to discuss the possibility of advertising them in this portal to a West Coast focused audience.

The Ocean data portal team has a DRAFT Best Practices Guide that outlines some of the requirements for contributors. While minimal metadata is required, there are many pathways to inclusion. Contact us via the form below to learn more about what may work best for your information.
